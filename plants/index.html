Sean's Succulent Page

I love succulents. They’re great if you have a hard time keeping plants alive. They’re also great if you’re a plant enthusiast! Really they're just great.


But what are succulents exactly? Are they cacti? Are they trees?


A succulent is any kind of plant that stores water in its leaves, stem/trunk, and roots in order to survive in arid climates. So ALL cacti are succulents, though not all succulents are cacti. There are even a few trees that are succulents. Here's an example of a Baobab tree. A very large succulent!

Succulents are also SUPER popular these days. With our broken economy and millennials living in basements and tiny apartments succulents have become the "go-to" plants. You don’t even need to visit a greenhouse or nursery anymore to buy one because grocery stores sell them too. But let’s be real; many people get clippings from friends. This works well because many succulents are easy to propagate!
